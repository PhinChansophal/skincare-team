Pot Vongsocheata

Leader – Skincare Project
Building a practical and modern skincare product platform with a real-world focus.

This project was initiated as part of a team assignment in Class A5 at the Royal University of Phnom Penh. Our goal is to showcase product filtering, responsive layout, and e-commerce-style interaction using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap.

It's more than just code — it's a creative learning experience combining design, user interaction, and real data handling. The platform is also inspired by modern UI/UX standards for small business or startup use.
